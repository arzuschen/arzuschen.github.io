About Me

RZ comes from the pinyin "ruo zhi" (a childhood nickname I have kept for years), and can also be written as Arzie, or Arzus in more formal settings. It started as a self-deprecating reminder of how much I had to learn, and as a belief that diligence can make up for what talent does not. I registered this domain back in school, hoping to build a personal website while I was searching for my first job. I ended up leaving it unused for a long time after getting hired, but it now feels like the right time to start writing here and use this space to keep track of my thoughts.
